About Me

I am an enthusiastic entrepreneur with a business education (undergrad at UNC Kenan-Flagler) and market research experience (3 years at System1 Research). During my first full-time job in market research, I directed the development of a customizable app version of the popular game Catchphrase and launched “Words For Us”. I soon jumped full-time into entrepreneurship to co-found Fisher Guiding in Spring 2017. After managing product development for a few years, I decided to learn coding as well to add to my entrepreneurial toolkit. A few of my other passions are playing and watching sports, volunteering at By The Hand Club For Kids, and listening to music during most things I do (here’s what I'm listening to on Spotify).
